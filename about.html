El Shaddai Altar Servers

The Ministry of Altar Servers is group of young servers assisting in the Parish Liturgical and Religious celebrations. Its main goal is to encourage young men and women to become worthy guards of honor to the Eucharistic King and to enkindle greater piety and devotion to Christ through the Holy Sacrifice of the Mass.

Mission:

To involve young men in serving Jesus through the Sacred Liturgy of the One, Holy, Catholic, Apostolic Church and the worship of His words and works.

Objectives:

To develop young men in ministry to Jesus and His Church;
To strengthen their relationship with Jesus;
To create a strong brotherhood using service as foundation;
To engage in social, cultural, sports, and religious activities;
To help in the evangelization efforts initiated by the parish.
